About Us

Who we are ?

Geer Information Technology and Communication Company is a leading company provides Communications and information technology services that covers the entire technological, operational, tactical, and strategic of customer requirements. Geer has a variety of products and services that covered all Cabling, Networking, UPS, CCTV solutions. Since its establishment Geer was capable of delivering a complete technological and operational solution to its customers, such as Government, Healthcare, Construction, Banking, Manufacturing.

Our Vision

Geer vision is to provide our customers with reliable ICT solutions from beginning to end, bridging the gap between technology innovation and local market needs.

Our Mission

Geer mission is to provide high quality ICT service tailored to meet customer requirement.
