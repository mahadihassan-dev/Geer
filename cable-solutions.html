Structured Cabling Solutions

Protect your systems, data and operations with our uninterruptible power supply system.

Geer provides high performance network infrastructure that can make big difference between business success and failure.   • Pre Terminated Copper and Fiber Solutions.
  • Horizontal/Vertical Cable Management
  • Intelligent Infrastructure Solutions
  • Cable Runway
  • Network and Servers Cabinets
  • Rack Systems & Components, PDU & Power strips
