CCTV Solutions

We make security as you need it to be

With our CCTV products we guarantee you that you get the maximum security and protection out of your surveillance system. We are offering a broad range of products which utilize the latest advancements in IP camera technology. Geer IP network solutions are innovative, flexible, and powerful, and have proven effective in protecting businesses and organizations of all shapes and sizes.

What is IP camera?

IP cameras are typically equipped with an embedded web server and can be accessed and controlled over any IP network such as a WAN, LAN, internet. IP cameras are easier to install than analog cameras because they do not require a separate cable run or power boost to send images over a longer distance.

Dome CCTV Camera:

Used for indoor security and surveillance applications. The dome shape makes it difficult to tell the direction that these cameras are facing. it is ideal for deterring criminals.

Bullet CCTV Camera:

Used in applications that require long distance viewing. A bullet CCTV camera is a wall-mount or ceiling-mounted unit that is typically designed for indoor and outdoor.

Night Vision CCTV Camera:

IR illumination makes the camera ideal for dim environments or even in complete darkness.

PTZ CCTV Camera:

Geer offers both Network and HDCVI PTZ cameras. PTZ cameras use pan, tilt, and zoom functionality to provide both wide-area coverage and great detail. Specialized cameras, like positioning systems, Hybrid Network PTZ (Temperature Measurement with Thermal Sensor Tech), anti-corrosion cameras, and explosion-proof cameras are also available for user special applications. The Ultra Series of Geer cameras feature high performance sensors, powerful optical zoom, long range IR illumination, and powerful protection to satisfy applications with high performance demands. The Pro Series GEER Cameras focuses of on the balance between performance and cost, while Lite and Wi-Fi Series offers users alternatives with compact designs and affordable price. Geer PTZ cameras are widely used in different vertical industries like Government, Transportation, city surveillance, etc., and have proven their durability and effectiveness even in extreme conditions.
